Twilight in the Enchanted Forest

November 2023 · Unity/C#/Reaper · Individual

A 3D first-person adventure where you play as a lost traveller navigating a misty forest, designed as an intro sequence for a longer game.

A major goal was to explore atmospheric sound design with Reaper and Unity. The outcome features 3D audio for various terrain, an ambient forest soundscape, and sound effects for props and player movements.

I also aimed to design a visually immersive environment - inviting the player to explore while also preserving the mystery of the forest.
